	19:20 - 19:40 - Jens Roed Andersen, 15 min.
	
	Jens Roed Andersen is what can be called "first generation cyberwarrior" and a real oldtimer, as he started working in information security 25 years ago. Initially as the CISO for Arla Foods and Energinet.dk and since as freelance consultant, with many Danish and foreign clients on his list. Recently, he has been focusing on OT security and the secure SDLC, particularly how to produce secure code, while at the same time staying competitive.
His talk will be on his experiences on using storytelling as a means to explaining this quite complex area to outsiders. The somewhat short history of cybersecurity has shown though, that one of the biggest obstacles is in fact not necessarily technical, but more in direction of explaining the "why, what and how" to the money men/women in the business/management.
